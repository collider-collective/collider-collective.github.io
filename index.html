(pink) Collider. Sophie Spence (born Victoria, Australia), Zach Micallef (Victoria, Australia) Maud Freeman (Queensland, Australia) & Adrian J Song (Shah Alam, Malaysia) are artists living and working on the unceded land of the Boon Wurrung & Woi Wurrung language groups of the Eastern Kulin Nations. Together they Formed the Collider artist collective in late 2020, as a means to question, examine, and discuss the ongoing violence of colonisation.
