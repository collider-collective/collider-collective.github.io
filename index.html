Collider

COLLIDER was formed in 2020 by Adrian Jing Song Maud Freeman, Sophie Spence, and Zachariah Micallef.

COLLIDER operates on the unceded land of the Woi Wurrung and Boon Wurrung language groups of the Eastern Kulin Nations. COLLIDER accepts the continued responsibility we, and other non-Indigenous people share in actively unlearning settler histories. We reject our ancestors’ assertion of ‘terra nullius’ and acknowledge that sovereignty has never been ceded, and pay our respects to Elders past, present.

COLLIDER also acknowledges First Nations People who were forcibly removed or otherwise displaced from their families and traditional lands, who now call the Kulin Nation’s home.
